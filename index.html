Ophthalmic Artificial Intelligence From Data Integration to Accountable Clinical Impact: Challenges and Opportunities

Tobias Schlosser, Michael Friedrich, Richard Vogel, Benny Platte, Matthias Vodel, Danny Kowerko, Marc Ritter, Miriam Goldammer, Markus Wolfien

Ophthalmic AI is moving beyond image classification toward multimodal interpretation, prediction of disease course or treatment need, and clinical workflow integration. The central challenge is turning technical performance into accountable clinical value.

Central finding

Technical performance is no longer the only bottleneck.

Accountable value depends on interoperable data, representative validation, transparency, fairness, privacy, cybersecurity, regulation, workflow fit, cost-effectiveness, and post-deployment monitoring.
