Validation notes

Known, documented limitations
  • 1 included study is absent from the 426-row merged extraction table: schlosser2024visualacuity. Its final LaTeX table and BibTeX data are retained without fabricated enrichment.
  • 1 record(s) have no abstract in the normalized metadata; no abstract was invented.
  • Local full-text files are not published unless an explicit CC BY or CC0 licence and a structured full-text source are both present; unknown licences default to a restrictive mode.
  • Machine-readable node/edge data for the keyword network and country-level collaboration counts are not present; the existing authored figures are exposed with descriptive alternatives, not reverse-engineered values.

Licence boundary

Website code, derived metadata, manuscript text, authored figures, and third-party publication content can have different rights holders and licence conditions. The downloadable metadata and audit tables do not grant rights to third-party full text. See the repository's licence note for the category-specific policy.
