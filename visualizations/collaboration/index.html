Manuscript caption: Global distribution of publications and international co-authorship in the included literature on artificial intelligence in ophthalmology. Countries are shaded according to the number of publications, and links indicate international co-authorship relations, with stronger links reflecting more frequent collaboration. The map shows that publication activity and collaboration are concentrated in a limited number of countries, with the strongest publication output and collaboration links centered on the United States, China, and India, while other world regions remain comparatively underrepresented.
