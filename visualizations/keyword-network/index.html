Manuscript caption: Keyword co-occurrence network of the included literature on artificial intelligence in ophthalmology. (A) Network visualization, in which node size reflects keyword frequency, line thickness indicates co-occurrence strength, and colors indicate data-driven keyword clusters interpreted into thematic areas. Visible labels denote prominent terms, whereas unlabeled nodes represent less frequent connected keywords. (B) Overlay visualization by average publication year, highlighting temporal shifts in research attention. Four major thematic areas can be discerned: core AI methods and computational approaches; OCT-based retinal imaging and biomarker analysis; retinal disease diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment monitoring; and screening, clinical workflow, and emerging translational applications.
