How to read these views

The evidence-gap maps summarize how publications frame each dimension. They do not represent pooled effect sizes, diagnostic-accuracy estimates, risk-of-bias ratings, or certainty-of-evidence assessments. Counts in multi-label charts are article-level assignments and may exceed the number of included publications.
