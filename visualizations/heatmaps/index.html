Task, target, and method heatmaps

Two multi-label matrices show where task families intersect with clinical targets and where method families intersect with tasks. Every cell retains its explicit assignment count.

Assignments, not independent studies

One publication may contribute to multiple cells when it addresses several tasks, targets, or method families. Cell counts are literature-record assignments and must not be summed as mutually exclusive publication totals.
